## Presentation Design Guidelines

### Visual Consistency:
- **Color Scheme:** Green (#4CAF50), Blue (#2196F3), White (#FFFFFF)
- **Typography:** Roboto for headers, Open Sans for body text
- **Icons:** Material Design icon set
- **Charts:** Consistent color coding and styling

### Slide Layout Principles:
- **Maximum 6 bullet points per slide**
- **Large, readable fonts (minimum 24pt)**
- **High contrast for accessibility**
- **Consistent spacing and alignment**
- **Professional imagery and graphics**

### Interactive Elements:
- **QR codes for demo access**
- **Clickable prototype links**
- **Video demonstrations (if presenting digitally)**
- **Live dashboard showcase**

### Presentation Flow:
1. **Hook:** Strong opening with problem statistics
2. **Solution:** Clear value proposition
3. **Technical:** Detailed implementation approach
4. **Validation:** Prototype demonstration
5. **Business:** Feasibility and market analysis
6. **Impact:** Social and environmental benefits
7. **Future:** Scalability and sustainability
8. **Close:** Strong call to action

This extended presentation structure provides comprehensive coverage while maintaining engagement and clarity for the SIH 2025 judges and stakeholders.
